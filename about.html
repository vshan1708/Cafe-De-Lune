Our Story

Café de Lune was born from a simple idea: that the quiet magic of the night deserves a place of its own. Inspired by moonlit walks, late-night thoughts, and the comforting aroma of freshly brewed coffee, we created a space where warmth meets elegance, day or night. Nestled in the heart of the city, our café offers a serene escape from the noise, blending minimalist design with rich flavors. Every cup we serve and every dish we plate is a tribute to detail, atmosphere, and soul. Whether you're here for an early espresso or a late dessert under the stars, Café de Lune invites you to pause, breathe, and savor the moment.

Our Mission

To create a peaceful escape for night owls by combining elegance, comfort, and creativity in every cup.

Our Vision

To be the most loved night-time café in Kuala Lumpur, where every visit feels like a moonlit moment.
